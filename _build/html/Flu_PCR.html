Equation of the mean#

\[\bar{x} = \frac {x_1 + x_2 +... + x_n}{n} \tag{1} \label{mean} \]

Standard deviation of the mean#

\[ s = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N-1} \sum_{i=1}^N (x_i - \overline{x})^2} \tag{2} \label{Sdv} \]
